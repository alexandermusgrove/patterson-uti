Why Glean

Why Glean?

Patterson-UTI is facing a common enterprise AI challenge: demand is established, but usage is fragmented across sanctioned tools, limited-license environments, and shadow AI behaviors.

Today, employee use of LLM assistants, whether in corporate Gemini licenses or unsanctioned tools, creates a fragmented operating environment for the business and a governance burden for IT. Glean gives Patterson-UTI a way to standardize that environment with one horizontal AI layer across the existing stack.

Patterson-UTI knowledge sits across field procedures, rig history, safety content, contracts, prior project work, and office systems. Glean connects that enterprise context with permissions intact so employees can retrieve the right answer faster and use it inside search, assistant, and agent workflows.

Enterprise AI only becomes useful when outputs are grounded in real company context and easy for employees to trust. By pairing search, assistant, and agents with permission-aware enterprise knowledge, Glean improves answer quality and drives stronger day-to-day adoption than generic copilots alone.

Rather than adding another point solution, Glean gives IT a governed AI layer for search, assistant, and agents. That creates a durable foundation that can reduce tool sprawl, support future transformation, and improve time-to-answer across both field and office workflows.
